Artist Bio

History

Began writing music in 1998, taking up turntablism in 1999, studying Advanced Sound Engineering at Pulse Recording Sudios in 2004. Began learning bass in 2008, then began studying Guitar in 2009.

Performed DJing gigs in the Temple Bar Music Centre aka The Buton Factory multiple times for various promoters.

Played resident for Human Music in Mischief on South William Street

Performed for Techno Warfare

Played in The Vaults nightclub

Took part in multiple DJ competitions around the Dublin area

  • Vestax PDX-D3S mkII - Discontinued
  • Vestax VCI-380 - Discontinued
  • Roland AIRA TR-8, TB-3, and MX-1
  • Fender Aerodyne Jazz Bass
  • Fender Stratocaster Buddy Guy signature guitar
  • Gibson Les Paul Gary Moore BFG signature guitar
  • Yamaha HS8 + Sub
